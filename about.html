About MediWorld (Pvt) Ltd

Since 2001, Mediworld (Pvt) Ltd has been providing various types of equipment and accessories. We are an importer and supplier. We are a trusted provider of innovative medical equipment solutions for healthcare. We are committed to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our History

Established in 2001, MediWorld has a rich history of providing high-quality medical equipment. Work with Millennium Specialised Hospital, BIHS Hospital, Diabetic Association Medical College Hospital, Grace Hospital, Mazdi City Hospital, Rehabilitation & Vocational Training Centre (RVTC), and many more.
